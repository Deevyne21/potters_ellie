Start Your Child’s Journey with Distinct Destiny

Admissions at Distinct Destiny are designed to be simple, transparent, and welcoming. We value each student as an individual and look forward to partnering with parents to help their children thrive academically, socially, and emotionally.

Our Admission Process

Joining Distinct Destiny is easy. Here’s a simple guide to our admissions process and how your child can start their learning journey.

Inquiry

Reach out to our admissions office via phone, email, or the online form to express interest.

Visit / Tour

Schedule a visit to see our facilities, meet our teachers, and experience the school environment.

Application Submission

Complete the admission form and submit the required documents.

Assessment / Interview

Depending on the stage (Nursery, Primary, or Secondary), students may complete a brief assessment or interview to help us understand their learning needs.

Acceptance & Enrollment

Once accepted, parents receive an enrollment package with instructions for the next steps, including orientation and class placement.

Admission Requirements

We aim to make the process simple while ensuring we understand each child's needs:

Completed Admission Form

Fill out all required fields accurately

Birth Certificate or Age Verification

Valid proof of child's date of birth

Recent Passport Photographs

Two recent passport-sized photos of the child

Previous School Records

For Primary and Secondary applicants only

Immunization Records

For Nursery and Primary applicants only

Need Help?

Our admissions team is here to guide you through every step. Contact us if you have questions about the required documents or need assistance with the application process.
